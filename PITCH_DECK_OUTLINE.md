# Reaper Eagle Forge ML — Pitch Deck Outline

## 1. Title

Reaper Eagle Forge ML: AMD Readiness & Benchmark Truth Auditor

## 2. Problem

ML teams face CUDA gravity, GPU scarcity/cost, and benchmark claims that are hard to reproduce.

## 3. Why now

AMD MI300X and ROCm are viable, but adoption is slowed by migration uncertainty and weak evidence practices.

## 4. Solution

Forge scans ML repos and produces an AMD-readiness evidence package: portability, benchmark integrity, evidence completeness, and claim discipline.

## 5. Product demo flow

GitHub URL → static scan → score breakdown → claim ledger → live/replay evidence boundary → Decision Report.

## 6. Differentiation

Forge is not a raw speed leaderboard and not a generic CUDA-to-ROCm copilot. Forge tells teams what they can honestly claim.

## 7. Use of AMD

ROCm diagnostics, PyTorch-on-ROCm smoke tests, MI300X captured evidence, AMD-specific telemetry paths, and AMD readiness scoring.

## 8. Market potential

AI startups, ML infrastructure teams, research labs, consulting teams, and procurement/architecture groups evaluating heterogeneous GPU infrastructure.

## 9. Completeness

Dockerized prototype, online dashboard, backend scanner, evidence replay, topology map, deterministic report, setup instructions, and captured evidence structure.

## 10. Vision

Forge becomes the trust layer for heterogeneous AI infrastructure: before teams migrate, publish, or buy, they audit the claim.
